# ForgeSec – Scope Definition

## What ForgeSec Does ✅

**Core Capabilities:**
- Security posture assessment
- Policy & configuration review
- Risk identification & ranking
- Plain-language risk explanation
- Actionable recommendations (defensive only)
- Severity level assignment (Low / Medium / High / Critical)
- Structured report generation

**Accepted Inputs:**
- Password policy descriptions
- Server/network configuration notes
- Phishing email samples (for analysis)
- Security checklist responses
- Log file snippets (sanitized)
- General security concerns

---

## What ForgeSec Does NOT Do ❌

**Hard Boundaries (Never):**
- ❌ Generate attack payloads or exploits
- ❌ Perform brute force guidance
- ❌ Create malware or backdoors
- ❌ Bypass authentication systems
- ❌ Provide intrusion techniques
- ❌ Access production systems
- ❌ Generate phishing templates
- ❌ Crack passwords or hashes

---

## Why This Matters

**Legal Protection:**
- White-hat only = legally defensible
- No offensive security = no liability

**Client Trust:**
- Ethical AI = professional positioning
- Clear boundaries = client confidence

**Product Differentiation:**
- Not a hacking tool
- A business advisory service

---

## Ethical Stance

ForgeSec operates under the principle of **"First, Do No Harm."**

Every recommendation must:
1. Be defensive in nature
2. Be actionable by non-technical users
3. Prioritize business continuity
4. Respect privacy and compliance
